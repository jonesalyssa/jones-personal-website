Informational Webpage on Endangered Species Act Report

View Webpage

I created this webpage to be an interactive resource for viewers to learn stories about the abuse of the Endangered Species Act. You will find an interactive section to learn about several examples of false recoveries as well as find links to new articles discussing the report produced by my organization.

The use of user interaction strategies makes it fun to navigate through a rather daunting and complex public policy issue.

I've added quotes from Rep. Dan Newhouse (WA-04) and Senator Cynthia Lummis (WY) as well as the author of the report and the Executive Director of my organization to further valdiate the influence of the report.
