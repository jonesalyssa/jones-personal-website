ABOUT ALYSSA JONES

Alyssa Jones is a young professional from southwest Virginia working in Communications, Digital Marketing, and Digital Production with professional interests peaking in video production, brand marketing, and audience engagement in Washington, D.C. She is from Ridgeway, Virginia and graduated from Virginia Tech in May 2023 with Degrees in National Security & Foreign Affairs, Criminology and Sociology with minors in Peace Studies, International Relations, and War & Society. She is currently furthering her education as a student in a web development program through her alma mater that will conclude in March 2025.

Alyssa blends her technical and creative skillset with her knowledge of national security issues to help organizations craft branding and marketing materials that will draw interest to their brand and message. With a career exclusively working for non-profits and campaign organizations, Alyssa thrives in taking her understanding of complex policy issues to create needed brand assets for a variety of organizations.

Alyssa is the Communications Manager (NGO) in Washington, D.C. As a department lead, she maintains a diverse workload including videography, photography, post-production editing, graphic design, social media management, high-traffic email cultivation, web development, stakeholder relations, and planning events of high importance on Capitol Hill and throughout the nation.

Alyssa serves as the Social Media Director for a top-grossing Political Action Committee at a part-time capacity. Her role focuses on crafting the messaging around key public policy issues while generating brand awareness through various campaigns.

The most rewarding capacity in which Alyssa services is as a co-producer of “Champions of Rural America,” a live-to-air television series airing monthly on RFD-TV featuring members of the Congressional and Senate Western Caucus along with key voices in western and rural industries.

Alyssa is also a freelance Digital Producer with clients seeking services video production, photo editing, and graphic design. Her diverse skillset also allows her to assist with other creative production through email marketing, web design, and social media management.
